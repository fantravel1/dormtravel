👋 About Us

We're DormTravel

The world's most fun travel resource built exclusively for college students. No parents. No mixed-age content. Just students.

🎯 Our Mission

Empowering Students to Travel Smarter, Safer & Louder

DormTravel exists because college travel is different. It's not about luxury resorts or retirement cruises. It's about cramming 6 friends into an Airbnb, finding $5 street food in a new city, and creating the stories you'll tell at every reunion for the rest of your life.

We're not a booking site. We're not trying to sell you anything. We're a knowledge and inspiration hub that helps you answer: Where should we go? How do we afford it? What should we know before we go?

Every destination guide, budget tip, and safety resource on DormTravel is written specifically for the college experience. Because your trip to Cancun with your roommates is nothing like your parents' anniversary vacation.

💪 What We Believe

Our Core Values

🎓

Students First, Always

Every piece of content is written for current college students. Your budget, your vibe, your timeline. Not your parents' travel guide.

🔒

Safety Without the Lecture

We keep it real about safety without being preachy. You deserve honest information about risks, consent, and smart decision-making.

💰

Budget Transparency

Every destination includes real cost breakdowns. No hidden fees, no "starting from" BS. If you can't afford it on a student budget, we'll tell you.

🌎

Experiences Over Things

We celebrate the moments, not the luxury. The best travel memories are made in hostels, on road trips, and at 2 AM in a city you'd never heard of before.

👥

Inclusive & Welcoming

DormTravel is for every college student regardless of background, identity, or experience level. First-time traveler? Perfect. We're here for you.

🚀

No Exploitation

We never promote exploitative tourism, unsafe party culture, or anything that puts students at risk. Fun and responsibility aren't opposites.
