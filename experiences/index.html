🏆 Must-Do List

College Bucket List Experiences

The experiences every student needs before they graduate. How many have you done?

1 🏝
Spring Break with Your Crew
The defining trip of your college years. Beach, party, bonding. Pick a destination and go.
2 🎓
Study Abroad Semester
Live in another country for 4+ months. Biggest growth experience in college. Period.
3 🚗
Cross-Country Road Trip
Best friends, open road, epic playlist. Drive coast to coast at least once before you graduate.
4 🎵
Music Festival Trip
Coachella, Bonnaroo, Lollapalooza, or a local fest. Camp, dance, and make friends with strangers.
5 🌎
Euro-Trip Backpacking
Hostels, trains, 5+ countries in 3 weeks. The ultimate post-semester adventure.
6 🌞
Tropical Island Getaway
Bali, Thailand, Puerto Rico, or Hawaii. Disconnect from campus life and recharge.
7 ⚽
Big Game Road Trip
Follow your team to an away game. Tailgate, cheer, and take over another school's town.
8 🎓
Graduation Trip
The last hurrah with your college crew. Go big. You earned it.

❓ FAQ

Experience FAQ

The most popular include spring break trips (Cancun, Miami), study abroad semesters (Barcelona, London), road trips (PCH, cross-country), music festivals (Coachella, Bonnaroo), backpacking adventures (Europe, Southeast Asia), and graduation trips. The best experiences combine adventure, friend bonding, and creating lifelong memories.

Every college student should try at least one of: a spring break trip with friends, a study abroad semester, a cross-country road trip, an international backpacking trip, a music festival, a graduation trip, a solo travel adventure, and a volunteer trip abroad. These experiences build confidence, create lifelong friendships, and provide stories you'll tell forever.
